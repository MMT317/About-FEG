About Freedom Equity Group

Founded in 2002 by Ron Bloomingkemper, Ron Petrinovich, and Bill St. Clair, Freedom Equity Group (FEG) is a nationally recognized Independent Marketing Organization (IMO) specializing in Indexed Universal Life (IUL) insurance and Fixed Indexed Annuities (FIA). Headquartered in Arroyo Grande, California with offices in Dallas, Texas, FEG partners exclusively with the highest-rated insurance carriers in the financial services industry. In 2024, FEG became a wholly owned subsidiary of F&G Annuities & Life, Inc. (NYSE: FG), a member of the Fidelity National Financial (NYSE: FNF) FORTUNE 500® family of companies.

FEG holds an A+ rating with the Better Business Bureau and has been in continuous operation for over two decades. The organization is committed to empowering American families to achieve financial independence through innovative, secure insurance and retirement solutions.

Fidelity National Financial (NYSE: FNF)

Fidelity National Financial, Inc. is a FORTUNE 500® company ranked #313 on the 2025 list, with $13.71 billion in annual revenue (2024). FNF is the nation's largest title insurance company through its underwriters—Fidelity National Title, Chicago Title, Commonwealth Land Title, Alamo Title, and National Title of New York. FNF is also a leading provider of annuity and life insurance products through its majority-owned, publicly traded subsidiary F&G Annuities & Life, Inc. (NYSE: FG).

Source: Fortune.com (2025); FNF Investor Relations; FNF Q4 2024 Earnings Report

F&G Annuities & Life (NYSE: FG)

F&G (the marketing name for Fidelity & Guaranty Life Insurance Company) is a leading U.S. provider of annuities and indexed universal life insurance, headquartered in Des Moines, Iowa. Originally incorporated in 1959, F&G has served over 900,000 people nationwide. F&G reported record gross sales of $15.3 billion for full year 2024, a 16% increase over 2023—with gross sales more than tripling since 2020.

Source: FNF Q4 2024 Earnings Report; RetireGuide.com; Annuity.org

F&G's Ownership of FEG

In October 2021, F&G initially purchased a 30% ownership stake in Freedom Equity Group, solidifying a multi-year strategic partnership. FEG contributed to F&G's rank as a top 3 middle market IUL provider by case rate, according to LIMRA. In 2024, F&G acquired the remaining interest, bringing its ownership of FEG to 100%—making Freedom Equity Group a wholly owned subsidiary of F&G Annuities & Life. F&G CEO Christopher Blunt cited the growth potential of FEG, particularly in the middle market and multicultural communities across the United States, noting that F&G has deployed nearly $700 million into owned distribution as a core part of its growth strategy.

Source: F&G Press Release, October 8, 2021; F&G Annuities & Life Q2 2024 Earnings Call; LIMRA U.S. Retail Individual Insurance Sales Reports

Product Focus

MMT Financial and Insurance specializes exclusively in two proven product categories:

Indexed Universal Life (IUL) Insurance — Permanent life insurance with cash value growth tied to market-driven indexes, providing upside potential with downside protection through guaranteed product floors. IUL products feature living benefits, flexible premiums, and tax-advantaged cash accumulation.

Fixed Indexed Annuities (FIA) — Tax-deferred retirement savings vehicles offering principal protection, growth potential linked to market indexes, and guaranteed lifetime income options without the risks of direct market participation.
